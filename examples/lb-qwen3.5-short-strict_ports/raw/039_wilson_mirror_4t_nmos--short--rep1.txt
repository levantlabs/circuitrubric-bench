* Improved 4-Transistor Wilson Current Mirror (NMOS)
* Topology: Two input transistors (M1, M2), two cascode/output transistors (M3, M4).
* M1/M2 form the basic mirror pair. M3 is the cascode for the reference branch.
* M4 is the high-output-impedance output transistor.
* Note: In a standard 4-transistor Wilson, M3 and M4 are often identical to M1/M2 
* but biased differently. Here we define them with explicit W/L ratios relative to M1.

M1 out1 in1 in2 bulk1 w=10u l=1u
M2 out2 in2 in1 bulk2 w=10u l=1u
M3 out1 in3 out2 bulk3 w=5u l=1u
M4 out2 in4 out1 bulk4 w=5u l=1u

.MODEL NMOS NMOS (LEVEL=1 VTO=0.7 KP=100u GAMMA=0.6 LAMBDA=0.05)
