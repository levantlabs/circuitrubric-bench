* Differential Amplifier: NMOS Inputs, Cascode PMOS Current-Mirror Load, Single-Ended Output
* Topology: M1/M2 (Input Pair), M3/M4 (Active Load Mirror), M5/M6 (Cascode Load)

* --- Device Definitions ---

* Input Pair (NMOS)
M1 out_in drain=INP gate=INP source=S bulk=S model=NMOS W=10u L=1u
M2 out_in drain=INN gate=INN source=S bulk=S model=NMOS W=10u L=1u

* Active Load / Cascode Current Mirror (PMOS)
* M3/M4 form the basic current mirror load connected to VDD
M3 out_in drain=VDD gate=M4G source=S_M3 bulk=S_M3 model=PMOS W=10u L=1u
M4 out_in drain=INN gate=M4G source=S_M4 bulk=S_M4 model=PMOS W=10u L=1u

* Cascode Devices (PMOS) to increase output resistance and bandwidth
* M5 cascodes M3, M6 cascodes M4
M5 out_out drain=VDD gate=M5G source=out_in bulk=S_M5 model=PMOS W=10u L=1u
M6 out_out drain=INN gate=M6G source=out_in bulk=S_M6 model=PMOS W=10u L=1u

* --- Biasing Nodes (External Drive) ---
* Note: Gate voltages for the mirror and cascode devices (M4G, M5G, M6G) 
* are assumed to be provided by an external bias circuit not included in this netlist.
* The bulk terminals of PMOS devices are tied to VDD as per standard IC design.

* --- Model Cards ---
