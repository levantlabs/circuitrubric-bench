* Note: Per polarity conventions for IC cascades/mirrors, the load devices (M3/M4)

* Given the instruction to define the topology with "active devices", the active mirror is used.
* If purely passive resistors are strictly intended, uncomment R_load1/R_load2 and comment M3/M4.
