* Improved 4-transistor NMOS Wilson current mirror
* IREF = reference-current input node
* IOUT = mirrored-current output sink node
* VSS  = ground / negative rail

M1 nG   nG   VSS VSS NMOS W=10u L=1u
M2 nX   nG   VSS VSS NMOS W=10u L=1u
M3 IOUT IREF nX  VSS NMOS W=10u L=1u
M4 IREF IREF nG  VSS NMOS W=10u L=1u

.MODEL NMOS NMOS (LEVEL=1 VTO=0.7 KP=200u LAMBDA=0.02)
