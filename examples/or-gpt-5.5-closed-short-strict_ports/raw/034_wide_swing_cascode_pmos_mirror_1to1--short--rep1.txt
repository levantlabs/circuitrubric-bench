```spice
.SUBCKT PMOS_WS_CASCODE_MIRROR_1TO1 VDD IREF IBIAS OUT
* IREF is the 1x PMOS mirror reference node.
* IBIAS is the wide-swing cascode-bias node; drive with same current as IREF for nominal wide-swing bias.

MREF  IREF  IREF   VDD   VDD   PMOS  W=10u   L=1u
MOUT  NOUT  IREF   VDD   VDD   PMOS  W=10u   L=1u
MBIAS IBIAS IBIAS  VDD   VDD   PMOS  W=2.5u  L=1u
MCAS  OUT   IBIAS  NOUT  VDD   PMOS  W=10u   L=1u

.ENDS PMOS_WS_CASCODE_MIRROR_1TO1

.MODEL PMOS PMOS (LEVEL=1 VTO=-0.7 KP=50u LAMBDA=0.02)
```
